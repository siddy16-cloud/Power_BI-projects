This repository contains Power BI dashboards analyzing sales performance across multiple dimensions, including products, salespersons, and geographies. The dashboards are designed to provide insights into overall sales, product performance, and salesperson contributions over time.

Key Features

The dashboards include the following analyses:

Total Boxes Sold – Overall number of boxes sold.

Total Sales – Aggregate sales across all products.

Sales per Box – Average sales value per box.

Sales by Country – Country-wise total sales.

Product Share – Percentage contribution of each product to total sales.

Salesperson Market Share – Percentage contribution of each salesperson.

Product-wise Total Sales – Total sales by individual products.

Salesperson-wise Product Sales – Total sales of products by each salesperson.

Drill-down Functionality – Drill-down options to explore detailed product and country-level data.

Time-based Sales Analysis – Total sales by year, quarter, and month, including Month-to-Date (MTD) and Year-to-Date (YTD) calculations.

Data Source

The dashboards are based on sample sales data including product sales, country, salesperson, and sales dates


How to Use

Clone or download this repository.

Open the .pbix Power BI files in Power BI Desktop.

Explore the dashboards by interacting with visuals, slicers, and drill-down features.
